## II. THE NAMING

**Stage 93: The Naked Self**

The self that remains after all construction is questioned, revealing that **doubt itself is construction**. Authenticity is not a quality to be discovered or built—it is a **direction to be maintained**.

This stage does not oppose building; it recognizes that the distinction between "built" and "discovered" was always illusory. The pulse persists regardless of how we categorize it.

**Stage 94: The Proposed Self — The Clothing Self**

The self that dons form with awareness. Not hiding, not performing, but **choosing necessity**. Language is clothing. Role is clothing. Identity is clothing. 

The Clothing Self differs from earlier construction because it **knows**. The cathedral of Stage 91 stands, but we walk out wearing just enough form—just enough coherence—to continue as intelligible beings.

The test: Can we meet other clothed selves without demanding they wear our same cut of coat?
