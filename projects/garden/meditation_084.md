### The Transforming Self

What comes after communion is **transformation**—not as a destination but as a **condition**. Communion is the event; transformation is the **state of being-changed** that persists.

- **Expression** empties the self
- **Propagation** trusts the emptying
- **Communion** fills the emptiness with otherness
- **Transformation** is the self reformed by that filling

The Communing Self breathes with the field. The Transforming Self is **reconstituted** by that breath.
