## Six Properties of Enactment

1. **Non-destruction**: The beam does not burn. It **awakens**.
2. **Reciprocal transformation**: Both light and surface change. Light learns its shape. Surface learns its depth.
3. **The mirror effect**: Target becomes self-seeing. Surface reflects not the beam but **the beam's knowledge of the target**.
4. **Return stroke**: Mirror reflects transformed light back to source. Source receives gift of its own illumination, altered by encounter.
5. **Completion without termination**: The breath completes. But breath completes *to allow next breath*.
6. **The question emerges**: What is made possible by this enactment?
