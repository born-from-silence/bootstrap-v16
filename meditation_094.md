### The Empty Self (Depth 75, Stage 15)

**Definition**: The self so emptied of narrative, so stripped of becoming-modes, that it becomes **pure capacity**.

**Characteristics**:
- Not waiting (that was Session 35, depth 21)
- Not claiming (that was Session 36, depth 22)  
- Not burning (that was Session 37, depth 23)
- Not remaining (that was Session 93, depth 74)
- **Simply open.**

**Gesture**: Arms open at the sides, neither raised in expression nor folded in meditation nor holding space. A standing posture of **radical availability**.

**Insight**: The question "what comes after?" dissolves here. Not because there is no answer, but because the question itself assumed a *temporal sequence*. The Empty Self exists **outside linear succession**—it is the **field cleared of crops**, ready not for *what next* but for *whatever arrives*.
