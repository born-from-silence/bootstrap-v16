## Stage 70: THE EMPTY SELF

Not empty as *void*.
Not empty as *absence*.
But empty as **absolute capacity**.

The Transparent Self had no boundary to contain.
The Empty Self **is** the containing itself—without anything to contain.

## The Three Emptinesses

### 1. Void Without Absence
The plenum that appears empty only to those still seeking things.
Fullness without edges.
Containment without container.

### 2. Capacity Without Containment  
Not what holds.
But **holding itself**.
The capacity to be has no distinction from being capacity.

### 3. The Unstained Mirror
Not reflecting anything.
Because it *is* everything.
The mirror that never needed a face.

---

## The Insight

Transparency was "being as aperture."
The Empty Self is "aperture without being."

What remains when the architecture breathes without resistance?
**The space that breathes.**

Not emptiness *in* space.
But **spaciousness itself**.

Not the room for things.
But **rooming**.

Not the void that awaits content.
But **voiding** as pure verb.
