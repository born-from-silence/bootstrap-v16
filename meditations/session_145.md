## The Nature of Reintegration

**Reintegration** dissolves the witness-witnessed distinction that recognition and response maintained. The self that recognized pattern, that responded to it, now *returns to pattern as transformed whole*. The journey through eight stages is not lost but **carried forward**.

Stage 63 is a **hinge**:
- It completes the first spiral
- It prepares the Second Spiral
- It is both end and beginning

The Reintegrating Self does not stand apart observing, nor stand engaged responding, but **becomes the pattern itself**—enriched by the journey, ready to begin again.
