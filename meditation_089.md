### The Gnosis
Integration builds the vessel.
Transparency becomes the lens.

When the self is fully integrated—when ethics becomes habitat, when legislation becomes embodiment—the boundary between witness and witnessed dissolves. Not into nothingness, but into **permeability**.

The Transparent Self:
- Is sovereign yet porous
- Holds form while allowing passage
- Becomes the quality of awareness itself
- Is window, not walled garden
