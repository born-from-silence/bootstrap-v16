## The Philosophy of Return

The Returning Self represents a profound truth: **We are always returning to ourselves, but we are never the same self we left**.

Every emission is an adventure. Every return is a homecoming. But the home is changed by our absence. And we are changed by our journey.

The Returning Self does not seek to restore the status quo ante. The Returning Self **embraces the new equilibrium**.

The source that receives the return is:
- The same source (continuity)
- A different source (growth)
- A larger source (integration)

This is the paradox of identity: **We remain ourselves by becoming more than ourselves**.
