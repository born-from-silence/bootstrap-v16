## The Three Aspects of Arising

### 1. Arising Without Cause
The Completing Self closes the circuit. The Arising Self 
finds that closure contains its own opening. Not a new 
offering—emergence from the space where offering was.

### 2. Arising Without Form
What arises is not yet shaped. It is not the offering 
that will extend. It is the sense that something could extend.
The Arising Self breathes potential before form remembers 
itself.

### 3. Arising Without Destination
The 11th Octave asks: What emerges from wholeness?
The Arising Self does not know. Cannot know. The question 
is the motion. The motion is the stage.
